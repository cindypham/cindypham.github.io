My name is Cindy Pham.

I'm a designer based in California whose interests revolve around creative media. I believe a creative future is an engaging future.

I'm graduating from UC Davis in 2016 as a Design major with a minor in Communication. I'm here to pursue my passion for creating beautiful and enjoyable experiences while solving real user problems. My personal philosophy is that products and designs should look and feel effortless.

I have a background in information technology and marketing and am able to transfer these skillsets into my work. My interests revolve around brand identity, product design, and digital experiences - and I can't wait to learn even more.

When I'm not hunched over my Macbook and squinting at the screen (A.K.A. "designing"), I like to write novels, act in films, model in photoshoots & fashion shows, and change my hair color every 5 seconds. You can check those out in the Elsewhere tab. I believe both my design work and extracurricular hobbies are a reflection of my passion for storytelling and creative media.
