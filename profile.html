Let me tell you a story. One day, while working at a biotech company, I told my supervisor that I was considering a career in design. "Doing what you like is good and all," he said, "but maybe you should consider something more practical."

A week later, I quit the company and began teaching myself design software from scratch.

I'd take passion over practicality any day.

My name is Cindy and I'm a visual designer. I specialize in user interface and experience, brand development, and product development and design. When I'm not hunched over my laptop and playing around with Photoshop, I'm either writing my dystopian novel or changing my hair color for the fifteenth time.

|| PHILOSOPHY

Beautiful and enjoyable experiences are better when they can solve real user problems. Products and designs should look and feel effortless. A creative future is an engaging future, so never, ever stop creating things.

And for the love of God, don't use Comic Sans.
