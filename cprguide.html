In an emergency, you need your hands free.

CPR Guide is a smartwatch app that sends vibrations for each compression needed to aid a CPR victim. I conceptualized, research, and designed the app with the goal of promoting wearable technology as a practical innovation necessary for healthcare. The number one priority for the user interface and experience was making sure that the user could go through the app quickly and effortlessly during an emergency situation.
