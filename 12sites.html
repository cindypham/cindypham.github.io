12 Months, 12 Sites

Concept: Concept, design, and prototype a website from scratch every month for a year.

I started 2018 as a designer hungry to learn everything and learn it fast. The best way I learn is through practice â€” so every month, I decided to do that with a side project. I challenged myself to create a website at the start of every month with a different concept and design style each time. Many of the site concepts revolved around countries I traveled, books I'd been reading, and movies I'd watched that month. The rapid succession of each site allowed me to let go of perfection and experiment with Flinto, Principle, After Effects, and Cinema 4D. Once I finished, I compiled them together into a reel.
