LET'S GIVE DESIGNERS SOMETHING TO TALK ABOUT.

The Design Career Fair at UC Davis is an annual event that aims to expose design students to creative firms and mentor relationships while developing confidence as a designer. I led a group of 15 designers to conceptualize and design a branding identity from scratch that would promote the energy and community of UC Davis designers. We conveyed the theme, [insert future here], by utilizing bold brackets and an eye-catching CMYK color palette.
